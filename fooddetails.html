MAKING TEA

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. add tea leaves and sugar
  3. let it simmer for a few minutes
  4. strain the tea into a cup and add milk if desired

MAKING PARATHA AND ALOO CURRY

  1. dought for paratha and let it rest
  2. Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee
  3. for aloo curry,saute onions,tamatoes and spices in a pan
  4. Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickens

MAKING BIRYANI

  1. Marinate the meat or vegatable with pieces
  2. cook basmathi rice until its 70% done
  3. Layer the marinatred meat/vegatables and rice in a pot
  4. cook on a low heat until fully cooked
